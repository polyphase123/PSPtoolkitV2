Chapter 01

Overview of the 2026 Amended CSP Rules

On April 1, 2026, the Energy Regulatory Commission (ERC) enacted the Amended Implementing Guidelines for the Procurement, Execution, and Evaluation of Power Supply Agreements (PSAs) under Resolution No. 07, Series of 2026.

This amendment introduces structural transparency to distribution utilities (DUs) procuring electricity. By integrating strict timelines, joint DU-GenCo filing responsibilities, and citizen observers, the ERC guarantees that all contracts align with the principles of least-cost supply and fair market competition.

Regulatory Foundation

All Power Supply Agreements entered into by Distribution Utilities for their captive market must go through a Competitive Selection Process (CSP) unless specifically exempted under guidelines defined in Department of Energy (DOE) circulars.

Ref: ERC Resolution No. 07, Series of 2026, Section 1 & Section 3
Chapter 02

TIMELINE & MILESTONES

Timelines under the 2026 guidelines are strictly binding. The entire CSP process, starting from the first publication of the Invitation to Bid (ITB) up to the final execution/award of the contract, must be completed within 180 calendar days. DUs that exceed this timeline face automatic penalties and risks of PSA disapproval.

Track the mandatory legal steps, public consultations, and evidentiary hearings after your joint PSA is filed with the ERC.

Phase 1: Case Filing & Intake

DU and GenCo submit the joint application package under Appendix A. ERC checks completeness and issues a Docket Number.

Phase 2: Jurisdictional Requirements

ERC issues an Order of Hearing. DU must publish this notice in general circulation newspapers once a week for two consecutive weeks.

Phase 3: Expository Presentation

Public presentation explaining the PSA rate components. Captive consumers can file formal petitions or interventions.

Phase 4: Evidentiary Hearings

Cross-examinations of utility planners and generation engineers by ERC lawyers to audit technical and financial assumptions.

Phase 5: Submission of Briefs

Parties submit their final summaries and arguments. ERC takes the case under advisement for rate computation audits.
